Thoughtful cards mailed seasonally

Cards Connect brings smiles through the mail. Older adults receive handmade cards created by caring volunteers several times a year — in fall, winter, spring, and on Valentine's Day. The messages are free, heartfelt, and always appreciated.

How it works

Here's what to expect when you sign up:

Enjoy throughout the year

Cards arrive four times a year: fall, winter, Valentine's Day, and spring.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know to get started.

Who can sign up?

You must be 62 or older and live in Minnesota. Cards Connect is free — there is never a cost to participate.

How often will I get cards?

Cards arrive four times per year: fall, winter holiday season, Valentine's Day, and late spring.

Who makes the cards?

Volunteers of all ages — from students to community groups — make every card by hand with care and creativity.
