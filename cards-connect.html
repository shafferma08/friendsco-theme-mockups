Cards Connect brings smiles through the mail. Older adults receive handmade cards created by caring volunteers several times a year — in fall, winter, spring, and on Valentine’s Day. The messages are free, heartfelt, and always appreciated.

Frequently asked questions

Have questions? Read the answers below.

Who can sign up for Cards Connect?

You must be 62 years or older, live in Minnesota, and enjoy receiving cards in the mail.

How often will I receive cards?

Cards are mailed four times per year: in the fall, during the winter holiday season, for Valentine’s Day, and in spring.

Who makes the cards?

Our cards are made by volunteers of all ages — from students to artists to community groups — each one created with care and love.

What if I move?

Please let us know your new address so we can continue sending cards to you. You can email companions@friendsco.org or call (612) 746-0737.

Still have questions?

Email jkapp@friendsco.org or call (612) 746-0737.
