Footer Layout Options

Three streamlined approaches prioritizing AP style, structural hierarchy, and high-contrast functionality for older adults.

Option 1: The Streamlined Core

The Concept: Moves the newsletter signup to a dedicated horizontal band above the main footer. The primary blue provides grounding visual weight and high contrast for white text.

Accessibility: Solid white text on a dark blue background ensures readability. Every link features a distinct > icon.

Option 2: The Action Split

The Concept: Separates actions from information. The top half uses a light background to highlight the newsletter signup and donate button. The bottom half organizes the links into clean columns.

AP Style Updates: Corrects address formatting (spells out "Avenue", uses "MN" for state) and standardizes "e-mail" to "Email".

Option 3: The Oversized High-Contrast

The Concept: Designed for maximum readability. It simplifies the links into a 2-column grid to reduce cognitive overload.

Accessibility: Largest tap targets of all options. Uses standard browser link focus behaviors combined with a large font size.
