Belonging starts here

At Friends & Co, we offer programs designed to reduce loneliness and build lasting connections. Whether you're seeking a friendly voice, a one-on-one connection, or somewhere to belong, you're in the right place.

What it means to be a part of Friends & Co

When you participate in any of our programs — whether you're enjoying a conversation, visiting a companion, attending a gathering, or exchanging greeting cards — you become a member of our community.

What you can access

  • Friendly phone chats on your schedule
  • One-to-one companion matching
  • Greeting cards and handwritten notes
  • Special events and gatherings
  • Our Among Friends newsletter, full of stories and updates
  • Ongoing support and more opportunities to connect
