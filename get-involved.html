Volunteering with Friends & Co

Our volunteers are the reason the programs work. You don't need special training or experience — just a willingness to listen and show up.

Every volunteer is screened, trained and supported by our team, and we match you thoughtfully so the friendship works for both of you.

Volunteer opportunities

Seven ways to help. Choose the one that fits your time and interests.

Coffee Talk volunteer

Answer the drop-in phone line and have friendly, 20-minute conversations on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Phone Companion

Call the same older adult each week and build a real, ongoing friendship.

Visiting Companion
