Header Design System Demo

Hover over Free Friendship Programs to see the accessible, older-adult friendly "Mega Menu" approach. Sub-menus flyouts are difficult to navigate for individuals with motor-impairments, so a Mega Menu groups complex categories logically without requiring precise diagonal mouse movement.

I have kept Volunteer, About, and Support as simple, large-hit-area dropdowns.
