Our programs

Free programs for older adults

All of our programs are free to participants and open to older adults across the Twin Cities. No referral required to get started.

Phone Companions

A weekly one-on-one phone friendship — paired with a dedicated volunteer for regular conversation and connection.

Learn more ›

Visiting Companions

In-person friendship visits with a volunteer — in your home or out in the community, whatever fits your life.

Learn more ›

Let's Do Coffee & Lunch

Monthly in-person gatherings at local spots around the Twin Cities — LGBTQ+ welcoming and open to all older adults.

Learn more ›
