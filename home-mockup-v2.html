Why Friendship

Friends & Co celebrates aging through community-led service that fosters meaningful connections for everyone. Social connection creates long-lasting benefits for:

Connection Supports Health and Longevity

Regular social connection can boost longevity and protect against chronic illness, reducing the risk of heart disease, stroke, cognitive decline, and premature death.

For older adults navigating life transitions, a trusted companion can be the difference between a lonely week and one filled with laughter.

23%
of older adults in Minnesota report feeling socially isolated. Our programs are designed to change that.

Peace of Mind for Families and Care Partners

When a parent, sibling, or neighbor is matched with a Friends & Co companion, the whole family feels the relief. A trusted check-in each week means someone's paying attention.

Our staff and volunteers are trained to recognize signs that something has changed, so you hear about it early.

85%
of family members report lower caregiver stress after their loved one joins a Friends & Co program.

Stronger, More Connected Neighborhoods

Each matched friendship is a thread in a wider community fabric. Our programs bring generations together, reduce isolation at scale, and strengthen the neighborhoods we all live in.

Free, community-supported programs mean nobody's priced out of connection.

5,200+
older adults served across the Twin Cities in the past year, thanks to 600 active volunteers.
