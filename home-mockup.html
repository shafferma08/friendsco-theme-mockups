Our Mission

Because growing older shouldn't mean growing apart.

Social isolation affects nearly a quarter of older adults in Minnesota. Friends & Co exists to change that.

We carefully match older adults with background-checked, trained volunteers for weekly visits, phone calls, group gatherings, and handwritten letters. Participants choose what works best for them, and the match grows from there.

Every program we offer is free, community-supported, and designed to make meaningful connection easy, accessible, and genuinely fun.

Friends & Co programs are open to older adults age 60 and older living in the Twin Cities metro area. There are no income, health, or background requirements to join as a participant. All programs are free.
