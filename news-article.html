How one Let’s Do Coffee event turned into a year of volunteering.

Ric Landers

Ric Landers initially discovered Friends & Co through a Let’s Do Coffee event designed for LGBTQ+ older adults seeking community.

He subsequently became deeply engaged, taking on multiple volunteer positions including event support and handmade card preparation.

“Helping others is the foundation of my life, and Friends & Co gives me the chance to do that.”
