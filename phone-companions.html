Build a friendship from the comfort of your home

Phone Companions pair older adults with background-checked, trained volunteers for meaningful one-on-one phone conversations. Together, you and your companion can decide on a schedule that works best for you, with most choosing to connect weekly.

Frequently Asked Questions

All the answers you need to get started.

Who qualifies?
You must be 62 or older and live in Minnesota or Western Wisconsin. This program is for older adults seeking meaningful phone-based companionship. (Not suited for dementia care).
What happens next?
A member of our team will reach out to schedule a friendly assessment call. This helps us understand your needs and match you with the best possible companion.
Is support available?
Yes! If you miss a call or haven't heard from your companion, please contact our office. We are here to ensure your connection remains consistent and enjoyable.
In-person options?
Phone Companions is remote-only. If you prefer face-to-face visits, please check out our Visiting Companions program below!
