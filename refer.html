Who can be referred?

Anyone aged 62 or over living in Minnesota. They don't need to be a client of any other service, and there's no cost at any point.

You can refer a parent, a neighbor, a client or a friend. We'll reach out to them directly and find the program that suits them best.

How it works

What happens after you refer

  1. 1

    You send us their details

    Name, contact details, and anything you think we should know.

  2. 2

    We get in touch

    One of our team calls to introduce Friends & Co and talk through the options.

  3. 3

    They choose a program

    No obligation. They join whichever program feels right — or none at all.
