What you’ll do

You’ll be matched with one older adult and call them each week from wherever you are. Unlike Coffee Talk, this is the same person every time — so the conversation builds, and a genuine friendship has room to grow.

Become a volunteer in three steps

Apply online

Complete a short volunteer application, including a required background check.

Complete training

We’ll prepare you for your first call and introduce you to your staff contact.

Get matched

We pair you based on interests, availability and shared life details.

What being a Phone Companion involves

One person, one call a week, an ongoing friendship.

A weekly call

You and your companion agree a time that suits you both. Most pairs talk once a week for around half an hour.

Just conversation

No care tasks, no counseling. You’re there as a friend.

Support when you need it

Your staff contact checks in regularly and is available whenever something comes up.

A real commitment

Because someone is expecting your call, we ask for a consistent weekly slot over several months.

Time commitment

A weekly rhythm that fits around you

Ready to apply?

About half an hour a week, from home, at a time you and your companion choose.
